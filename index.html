About HateyHatey

HateyHatey is a reliable delivery service committed to connecting people across Bangladesh. Whether you need to send furniture, documents, food, or medicines, we ensure your packages reach their destination quickly and safely. Our team works around the clock to provide fast, secure, and convenient delivery solutions tailored to your needs.

Our mission is to make delivery hassle-free and trustworthy for everyone. With a growing network of riders and state-of-the-art tracking technology, we keep you informed every step of the way.

Meet The Owner

Parvez Al Mumin is the passionate founder behind HateyHatey. With a vision to make delivery services faster, safer, and more accessible across Bangladesh, Parvez leads the team with dedication and innovation.
